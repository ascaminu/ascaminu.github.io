About Us

Who we are

The Association of Cameroonians in Umeå (ASCAMINU) is a non-profit and non-political association made of Cameroonians and other nationals who admire the Cameroonian way of life.

It is registered as an ethnic group in Sweden with registration number 8024484498 and headquarters in Umeå.

It was founded in 2008 by Cameroonians who lived in Umeå at that time and who deemed it necessary to maintain the Cameroonian culture even in the diapora. We meet once every month to relax and discuss common issues plaguing their lives in Umeå and those of their comrades back home.

Membership to the association is open to any one who who admire the Cameroonian way of life and is ready to abide by our constitution and its by-laws



Our mission

ASCAMINU has as its main objectives
  • To support each other in times of sorrow and happiness.
  • To serve as a melting-pot for all Cameroonians in Umeå.
  • To establish contacts with organizations having the same pre-occupations and objectives.
  • To enhance our welfare through sustainable micro-projects.
  • To propagate and promote the complex but beautiful socio-economic and cultural values of Cameroonians as a people and Cameroon as a nation.
  • To facilitate the demand for and supply of scholarships to Cameroonians.
  • To promote the exchange of services between the people and the institutions of Sweden and Cameroon.
